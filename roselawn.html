Roselawn Cottage

Welcome to Roselawn Cottage, a 3 bedroom, 1.5 bathroom home nestled in the heart of North East Portland.

Featuring a newly finished basement, new cedar double-plank privacy fencing, a fully renovated kitchen, bathroom, half bath, den, and office. This home has received an entire remodeling from the bones up, with new electric throughout, new plumbing throughout, a new roof, new windows, and new siding.

With care taken to retain the cottage's unique character, this home is a lovely example of Portland charm with the perfect level of modern comfort.
